Empowering Students Through Disaster Preparedness

Our Disaster Preparedness and Response Education System is designed to equip students and educators in schools and colleges with the knowledge, tools, and confidence to respond effectively during emergencies.

Our Mission

We aim to build a culture of safety and resilience by integrating disaster education into academic environments. Through interactive modules, role-based simulations, and real-time alerts, we help institutions prepare for natural and man-made disasters.

Key Features

  • Role-based login for students, teachers, and administrators
  • Interactive learning modules and quizzes
  • Emergency contact and alert system
  • Customizable disaster response plans
  • Real-time updates and notifications
