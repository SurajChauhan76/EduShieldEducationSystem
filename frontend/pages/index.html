Schools Are Unprepared for Disasters

Despite India's high vulnerability to natural disasters, most educational institutions lack
proper disaster preparedness protocols, putting millions of students at risk.

Paper-Only Guidelines

Emergency protocols exist only on paper without practical implementation or regular training.

Lack of Structured Education

No integrated disaster management curriculum to systematically educate students and staff.

Poor Drill Coordination

Infrequent and poorly coordinated manual drills that fail to instill practical preparedness.

Missing Localized Awareness

Students unaware of how to react during disasters specific to their geographic region.
